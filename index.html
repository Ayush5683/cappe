ABOUT US  

Serving since 2009

Our Story

A coffee shop, where every cup tells a story! Founded in 2009, we began as a small, cozy corner in the heart of the city, fueled by a passion for exceptional coffee and a desire to create a welcoming space for our community

Join us on this exciting journey as we strive to elevate your coffee experience, one cup at a time. Thank you for being a part of our story, and we look forward to serving you for many more years to come!

Our Vision

At COPPE, our vision is to create a vibrant community hub where coffee lovers can come together to share experiences, ideas, and inspiration. We believe that coffee is more than just a beverage; it’s a catalyst for connection, creativity, and conversation. Join us on this journey as we continue to brew connections.

  • A welcoming space for connection and conversation.
  • Finest coffee from ethically sourced beans.
  • Classic favorites and innovative seasonal drinks.
